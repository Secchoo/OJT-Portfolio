KMC Solutions Inc.

KMC Solutions helps companies build long-term offshore teams and operations in the Philippines and Colombia. It supports American, Australian, and European businesses by handling compliant employment, infrastructure, and people operations, without the need to set up a local entity. The platform brings together two integrated offerings: KMC TEAMS, which enables companies to hire and retain dedicated offshore professionals while maintaining full ownership of team direction, culture, and performance, and KMC SPACES, which provides these teams with beautiful, fully serviced, design-led offices across 36 sites in Metro Manila, Cebu, Clark, Bogotá, and Medellín. Through the KMC Partner Program, the company works with advisors and firms supporting international expansion by offering a trusted platform for offshore teams and workspace solutions.
